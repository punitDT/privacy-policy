Privacy Policy – MoodShift AI

Effective date: December 12, 2025

Last updated: December 12, 2025

⚠️ AI Disclaimer

MoodShift AI generates responses using artificial intelligence based on your voice input. These responses are for personal use only and may not always be accurate. They are not a substitute for professional advice, therapy, or medical guidance. Use your own judgment, and in case of a mental health crisis, contact your local hotline immediately.

1. Overview

MoodShift AI ("we", "our", "the app") is a voice-based mood shifting companion. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device Identifier

We generate a random, anonymous device ID (UUID) on first app launch to maintain conversation context between sessions. This ID:

  • Is generated once and persists until you uninstall the app
  • Is not linked to your name, email, phone number, or any personal account
  • Cannot be used to identify you personally
  • Is reset when you reinstall the app

2.2 Voice & Speech Data

  • We only access the microphone when you hold the Speak button
  • Your voice is processed securely and never stored
  • No recordings are saved on your device or sent anywhere
  • Responses are AI-generated and for personal use only, not professional advice

Speech is converted to text using on-device or Apple/Google speech recognition. The transcribed text is sent to our AI service to generate a response. AI-generated audio responses are streamed directly to your device and are never stored.

2.3 Conversation History

To provide contextual responses, we store:

  • Your last 4 messages and 4 AI responses (8 messages total)
  • Stored in Firebase Firestore, linked only to your anonymous device ID
  • Older messages are automatically deleted when new ones are added
  • Automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity

2.4 Local Preferences

Stored only on your device:

  • Language and voice preferences (language, voice gender)
  • Streak and usage statistics (current streak, longest streak, total shifts, active days)
  • Peace mode status (ad-free period)
  • Crystal voice activation status
  • Crash reporting preference
  • Session count
  • Onboarding completion status
  • In-app review status

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 AI Processing – Groq

What we send: Your transcribed text, conversation history, language preference

Purpose: Generate personalized mood-shifting responses

Privacy: Groq Privacy Policy

3.2 Text-to-Speech – Amazon Polly

What we send: AI-generated response text

Purpose: Convert text to natural-sounding speech (standard, neural, or generative voices)

Note: Audio is generated in real-time and streamed directly to your device. No audio files are stored.

Privacy: AWS Privacy Policy

3.3 Speech Recognition – Apple/Google

What we send: Voice audio while you hold the Speak button

Purpose: Convert your speech to text

Note: Processing may occur on-device or via Apple/Google servers depending on your device settings

3.4 Analytics – Firebase Analytics

What we collect: Anonymous usage data including:

  • Session count and app opens
  • Feature usage (2× Stronger mode, Crystal Voice)
  • AI token usage counts (for service optimization)
  • Text-to-speech character counts and voice engine type
  • Country (derived from device locale)

Purpose: Improve app performance and understand feature usage

Note: No personal information, voice recordings, or conversation content is collected

Privacy: Firebase Privacy

3.5 Crash Reporting – Firebase Crashlytics

What we collect: Anonymous crash data, device type, app version

Additional context: Voice gender preference, language setting, streak count, crystal voice status

Control: You can disable crash reporting in Settings

Privacy: Firebase Privacy

3.6 Advertising – Google AdMob

What is collected: Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID), device information

Ad types used:

  • Banner ads (bottom of screen)
  • Interstitial ads (every 4th shift)
  • Rewarded interstitial ads (for 2× Stronger and Crystal Voice features)
  • Rewarded video ads (for Peace Mode - ad-free period)

Control: Watch a reward video to activate Peace Mode and remove ads for 10 minutes

Privacy: Google Privacy Policy

3.7 App Configuration – Firebase Remote Config

What we collect: Anonymous app instance data

Purpose: Deliver app updates, feature flags, and force update notifications

3.8 App Security – Firebase App Check

What we collect: Device attestation tokens

Purpose: Protect our backend services from abuse and ensure requests come from legitimate app installations

Privacy: Firebase Privacy

3.9 Local Notifications

What we collect: Nothing - notifications are scheduled locally on your device

Purpose: Weekly reminder notifications to maintain your streak (if you grant permission)

Control: You can disable notifications in your device settings

4. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Conversation history Automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity
Local preferences Until you uninstall the app
Analytics data Per Firebase retention policy (14 months)
Crash reports Per Firebase retention policy (90 days)

5. Your Rights & Controls

  • Disable crash reporting: Settings → Toggle off crash reports
  • Delete all your data: Uninstall the app. This removes all local data and generates a new anonymous ID upon reinstall, permanently disconnecting you from any stored conversation history
  • Conversation history: Automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity
  • Limit ad tracking: Use your device's privacy settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking; Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads)
  • Disable notifications: Use your device's notification settings

For any other privacy concerns, contact us at moodshiftai.tech@gmail.com

6. Data Security

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Firebase services use Google Cloud security infrastructure
  • API keys and secrets are stored securely in Firebase Secrets Manager
  • Firebase App Check protects backend services from unauthorized access
  • No user accounts or passwords are collected
  • No audio recordings are stored - all audio is streamed in real-time

7. Children's Privacy

MoodShift AI is rated 9+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or collect identifying information.
